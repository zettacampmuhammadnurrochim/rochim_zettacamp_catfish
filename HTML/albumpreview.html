Sakura Saku Machi Monogatari, officially translated in the cover as The story of town where cherry blossoms bloom (sic), is the major debut album released by Ikimonogakari. The first press came packaged in a deluxe box with a special booklet. The album reached #4 on the Oricon weekly chart. Because it sold 158,101 copies in 2007, it was the #78 album of that year.
